About me

I am a former postdoc researcher and current open science enthusiast. I’ve recently finished a project on promoting and developing open science practices and computational reproducibility at Amsterdam University Medical Center.

If you’d like to speak about any of the above, or collaborate on an open data project, please get in touch!
